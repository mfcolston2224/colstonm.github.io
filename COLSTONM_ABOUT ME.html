About Me

I graduated from Oregon State University with a M.N.R. in 2022. In this graduate program my coursework is focused on sustainable natural resource management and stakeholder management (facilitation and conflict resolution) including public policy tools and processes. I also focus on the watershed resources management including forestry, water quality, and fisheries.

I also have a drone operator license and am always willing to map wetlands via photogrammetry and photoimagery.
